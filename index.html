What is Pluggy?

Pluggy is a smart charging assistant designed for busy offices, universities, and co-working spaces. When plug sockets are limited, Pluggy ensures everyone gets the power they need without the conflict.

Why Choose Pluggy?

Manage your battery efficiently and never worry about finding an available outlet at the last minute.

Smart Monitoring

Pluggy reads everyone's battery levels and usage speed in real-time to prioritize charging.

Custom Schedules

Tell Pluggy how many hours you need your laptop for, and it handles the rest.

Optimized Charging

Get notified of the best time to charge and when to stop to save electricity and help others.
